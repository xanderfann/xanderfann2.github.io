“Apple a day keeps the doctor away”

Xander’s Farm is an apple orchard with locations all over the country. We are family owned and started off from a continuation of a childhood dream. We are opened weekdays so pay us a visit or look at our homemade products. Stop by to pick your own apples in our orchard or take a tour to see how everything is made. We’re a favorite fall destination for families and groups alike and offer the ideal activities for a day of all fun! If you’re interested in apple picking we hope to have the opportunity to welcome you at Xander’s Farm this fall!
